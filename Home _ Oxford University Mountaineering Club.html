Oxford University Mountaineering Club

Welcome to the website of the Oxford University Mountaineering Club. Established in 1909, OUMC is one of the oldest and most active mountaineering clubs in the world. We climb regularly around the UK during term alongside our weekly schedule of indoor climbing and social events. Whether you're a fresher keen to take your first steps into the world of climbing or a seasoned mountaineer, OUMC welcomes everyone, students and non-students alike.

To keep up with all important club news, including sign-ups for meets and other events, join our mailing list by sending a blank email to climbers-subscribe@maillist.ox.ac.uk

This website contains (we hope) everything you might want to know about OUMC. If you've got a specific question our FAQ is a good place to start, or if you can't find an answer there feel free to contact the committee.
